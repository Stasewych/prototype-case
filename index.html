01 — Architecture

The Cost of
Convenience

Every extra plugin added milliseconds to the load time. The database was cluttered with metadata, and queries were slowing down.

"We made the strategic decision to strip away the heavy visual builders."

By re-engineering the core infrastructure on a custom WordPress theme using Vanilla JavaScript and optimized PHP templates, we regained complete control over the DOM.

02 — UX Engineering

The High-Friction
Paradox

Distress sales require detailed information (30+ data points). Conventional wisdom suggests short forms. However, we couldn't shorten the form without sacrificing lead quality.

Instead, we reduced the cognitive load. By integrating public property APIs, the system pre-fills house data as soon as the address is typed.

03 — Integration

Breaking Storage
Limits

Users needed to upload dozens of high-res photos. This routinely crashed standard servers.

We engineered a custom tunnel directly to the Google Drive API. Images upload straight to a structured Drive folder, instantly generating public links synced to Zoho CRM.
